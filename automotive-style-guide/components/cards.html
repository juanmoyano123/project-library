Card Design Guidelines

  • Border: All cards use 1px solid border (#151f26)
  • Padding: Standard padding is 32px for card content
  • Hover effects: Cards translate up 4-8px on hover with subtle shadow
  • Images: Use consistent aspect ratios (4:3 or 1:1)
  • Labels: Always include category labels in uppercase
  • Spacing: 24px gap between cards in grids
  • Background: White background on light pages, varies on dark sections
